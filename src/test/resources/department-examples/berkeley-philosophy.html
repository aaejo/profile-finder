Moses Hall, Unnamed

Moses Hall is no longer Moses Hall. For the time being it will be known as Philosophy Hall. This action, recently approved by University President Drake, was the initiative of graduate students and faculty in our department. More details at Berkeley News, in the San Francisco Chronicle, and in the Daily Californian.

Two New Distinguished Research Associates

We are very happy to announce the appointment of two very distinguished philosophers, Allan Gibbard and Kendall Walton, as Distinguished Research Associates in the Department of Philosophy. We look forward to their contributions to department life over the coming years in that capacity. Welcome Allan! Welcome Ken!
